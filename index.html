Welcome to CS Photography!

Are you a beginner photographer? Are you struggling to understand the basics of shutter speed, aperture, and ISO? If you are looking for a simple explanation and examples of these concepts you are in the right place! Go to the "Basics" page to learn the essentials of photography. Once you have mastered the basics you can move onto the "Star Photography" and "Water Drop Project" pages and learn how to take pictures that will impress anyone! You can also go to the "Portfolio" page and see examples of other photography and simple explanations of how to get pictures like those.
