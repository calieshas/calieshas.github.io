Website Analysis and Persona

Caliesha's Home Page

Downeast Basics

URL: https://www.downeastbasics.com/

Target Audience

Downeast Basics is targeting a wide range of female shoppers, however, their main audience is younger women striving to stay modest. A very large percentage of the shoppers at downeast will be between between the ages of 15-25. This site is very trendy and upbeat, but they keep it fairly simple. It is very easy to navigate the website and with the simple white baground it is not distracting from what they are trying to sell. Downeast Basics did a very good job and making their website enjoyable to use, it is smooth and doesn't take any hunting to find exactly what you are looking for.

Persona

Molly Norman

Molly Norman is a Student at BYU-Idaho and holds a part time job at Deseret Book. She is a 20 year old college student, born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah. She is also single and ready to mingle! Molly visits the Downeast Basics website multiple times a week to stay updated on the latest styles and sales going on in store and online. She is always on the lookout for great deals on modest clothing! Molly is very outgoing and loves socializing at activities around campus or on social media such as instagram and snapchat.
"I need a new modest dress for church!"

Site Contrast

Downeast knows exactly who their target audience is. They feature young women modeling their clothing on the home page so that young women who go to their website see those girls and imagine themselves in those clothes. They make shopping there very appealing! They meet the needs of their audience by making the site easy to navigate and very accesible to anyone. This website is not very "clicky", you don't have to travel to, and load, five different pages to get where you are going. The navigation list drops down and you just click once or twice on the things you are interested in, and you are where you want to go in an instant without having to load any other pages. It is a very good set up for mobile web surfing!
